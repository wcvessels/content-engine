# /setup (one-time brand + render configuration)

Run this ONCE when you adopt content-engine. It configures the durable, system-level facts: brand, design, voice, audience, render preferences. It writes your answers into `../_config/`, replacing every `{{PLACEHOLDER}}`.

This is ICM Pattern 8: setup collects facts that are true for EVERY deliverable. Per-document facts (the video, the title, the reader for THIS guide, which targets for THIS run) are collected conversationally by a lane's `01-ingest`, never here.

**Ships pre-filled.** content-engine ships with its own brand already in `_config/` as a working demo. You can ship deliverables immediately with the default brand, or run this to make it yours. Answer all questions in one pass (flat, no branching). Skip any question to keep the shipped default.

---

## How to run

1. Read each question below. Answer all of them in one message, or say "use the defaults" to keep the content-engine brand.
2. I write your answers into the `_config/` files, replacing the matching `{{PLACEHOLDER}}`.
3. I regenerate the wordmark SVG in your colors and fonts (a simple typographic wordmark, not an illustration).
4. I show you a one-page brand summary to confirm.

---

## Section 1: Identity  (writes `_config/identity.md`)

1. **`{{COMPANY_NAME}}` Brand name** as it should appear on documents. Example: `content-engine`, `Northwind Docs`, `Acme Field Ops`.
2. **`{{TAGLINE}}` Tagline**, one short line. Example: `Recordings in, documents out.`
3. **`{{BRAND_SENTENCE}}` Brand sentence**, one sentence on what you do. Example: `Acme turns field recordings into procedures every technician can trust.`
4. **`{{MISSION}}` Mission**, one or two sentences on why. Example: `Stop losing hard-won know-how in videos nobody rewatches.`
5. **`{{AUTHOR}}` Author / attribution** on each document. Example: `Will Vessels`, `Acme Knowledge Team`.

## Section 2: Design  (writes `_config/design-system.md`)

Give hex colors. If you give a brand color or two, I derive the rest to match.

6. **`{{PRIMARY}}` Primary color** (headings, links, the title-page rule). Example: `#2B3A8C`.
7. **`{{SECONDARY}}` Secondary color** (subtitles, captions, metadata). Example: `#5B647F`.
8. **`{{ACCENT}}` Accent color** (the one highlight, used sparingly). Example: `#E8A33D`.
9. **`{{BG}}` Background** and **`{{TEXT}}` text** colors. Example: bg `#FFFFFF`, text `#14171F`.
10. **`{{HEADING_FONT}}` Heading font** and **`{{BODY_FONT}}` body font**. The default brand is system-safe: `Helvetica Neue, Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif`. You may name a webfont, but no fonts are fetched at render, so pick faces with clean system fallbacks (the fallback is what most readers see).
11. **`{{SIZE_SCALE}}` Type scale**. Default is a 1.25 ratio on 16px. Say "default" unless you have a reason.

## Section 3: Letterhead  (writes `_config/brand/letterhead.md`)

12. **`{{LOGO_PATH}}` Logo**: I generate a typographic wordmark from your name + fonts + colors by default. Provide an SVG/PNG instead if you have one.
13. **`{{HEADER_TEXT}}` Eyebrow** (small line above the title on the title page). Example: your brand name, or leave blank.
14. **`{{FOOTER_TEXT}}` Footer**: the default is the static disclaimer line below. Say "default" to keep it.
15. **`{{DISCLAIMER}}` Disclaimer line**. Example: `Generated by {brand} from a recorded session. Verify against the source before relying on any step.`
16. **`{{CONFIDENTIALITY}}` Confidentiality line**. Example: `Internal use only.` or `Public.` (set per-deliverable too).

## Section 4: Voice  (writes `_config/voice.md`)  ::  answer with EXAMPLES, not adjectives

The no-em-dash rule and the jargon ban are FIXED and not configurable. Only these two are yours:

17. **`{{READING_LEVEL}}` Reading level**. Example: `around 8th grade, short sentences`.
18. **`{{TONE_WORDS}}` Tone**, shown as one good sentence in your voice rather than adjectives. Example of the content-engine tone: `Open Settings, then click Export. There is one catch: the file must be under 10 MB.` Give one or two lines that sound the way you want your documents to sound.

## Section 5: Audience  (writes `_config/audience-defaults.md`)

19. **`{{DEFAULT_READER}}` Default reader**. Example: `a new hire who was not in the recording`.
20. **`{{PRIOR_KNOWLEDGE}}` Assumed prior knowledge**. Example: `general computer literacy and nothing else; define every acronym`.
21. **`{{FORMAT_PREFS}}` Format preferences**. Example: `skimmable: clear headings, numbered steps, a screenshot next to each step`.

## Section 6: Render  (writes `_config/render-prefs.md`)

22. **`{{DEFAULT_TARGETS}}` Default targets**. Default: `PDF, HTML`. (DOCX is documented-only this build.)
23. **`{{PAGE_SIZE}}` Page size**. Default: `Letter`. Use `A4` outside the US.
24. **`{{TOC_DEPTH}}` TOC depth**. Default: `2` (heading levels 1 and 2).
25. **`{{HTML_EFFECT_CEILING}}` HTML effect ceiling**. Default: `restrained`.
26. **`{{FILE_NAMING}}` File naming**. Default: `{slug}.{ext}` from the document title.

---

## After you answer

I rewrite the `_config/` files in place, regenerate `brand/logo/wordmark.svg` in your colors and fonts, and confirm with a brand summary (name / tagline / palette / type). From then on every lane reads your brand automatically. Re-run this any time to rebrand; it only touches `_config/`, never a lane or a contract.
