# Fidelity checklist (pointer)

This lane runs the CANONICAL 16-item fidelity bar. It lives in one place and is not restated here, so the two cannot drift:

-> `../../../../_standards/qa-bar.md`

Run every item against the rendered SOP (PDF + HTML), the provenance sidecar, and the source manifest/transcript. For the SOP lane, the sharpest items are 4 (the source must actually state the claim), 6 (ZERO inferred content entries), 9 (owners named-only), and 10 (sequence honest). Every item must PASS; a fail is fixed at the right upstream stage and the bar reruns.

House rule: zero em dash characters.
